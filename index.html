We fight together

We are humans

We make a living by what we get, But we make a life by what we give. Service to others is the rent you pay for your room here on earth. We can change the world and make it a better place. It is in our hands to make a difference.

Change will not come if we wait for some other person, or if we wait for some other time. We are the ones we’ve been waiting for. We are the change that we seek. It is more rewarding to watch money change the world than to watch it accumulate.

You have not lived today until you have done something for someone who can never repay you. As you grow older you will discover that you have two hands. One for helping yourself, the other for helping others.

WE care about others

We are humans

We care about other because we are humans. We cannot see other in pain. If we see others in pain we alway want to help them. We can help them now with the help of donations. Your each donation will feed atleast one children in a society.

Peoples who are illterate will get educated with the help of your donations. Educating them can make their life better. There is nothing more beautiful than someone who goes out of their way to make life beautiful for others.

When you are kind to someone in trouble, you hope they’ll remember and be kind to someone else. And it’ll become like a wildfire. Think of giving not as a duty but as a privilege. The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others.
